About Me



Ed is a freelance photographer with a focus on the surf and ocean based aesthetic. His work is best described as a mix of action and art and has featured in advertising and editorial throughout Australia and internationally. His artistic yet systematic approach to the photographic process stems from a prior career in environmental science. Former pursuits in this field have had great influence over his perspective, knowledge and ultimately creative vision as he blends a critical approach with aesthetically driven work.
